In English

Kendo is a modern Japanese martial art of sword-fighting based on traditional samurai swordsmanship. Kendo is a physically and mentally challenging activity that combines strong martial arts values with sport-like physical elements. (http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Kendo)

Helsinki University Kendo Club was founded in 1998. Kendo is practiced actively but not.

How to get envolved? New to kendo? Helsinki University Kendo Club has beginners course starting each September and January. You need only sporting pants and a t-shirt to start with. The course lasts three months and costs 50 euros including insurance. We have many non-Finnish speakers in our club so don’t feel shy. Please contact our Treasurer prior to the course for details.

Visitors Helsinki University Kendo Club welcomes all visitors and people already familiar with kendo to join our practice! Please contact our Club President Otto Seppänen for details (otto dot seppanen (at) helsinki dot fi)

We practice thrice a week during the semester:

Tuesday 8:15am to 9:15am
Thursday 8am to 9am
Sunday 7:45pm to 9:15pm

The practice held at the Unisport - Administration Building (Hallintorakennus) in Fabianinkatu 20.
